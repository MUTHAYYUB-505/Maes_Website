About MAES

MAES, is a technology Company dedicated to continuously enhance its competencies in CCTV & Security System, Energy Efficiency to address the pressing needs of consumers to have secured facility, reliable power and to conserve energy. To achieve these objectives, theCompany provides solutions and services utilizing Power Quality, Energy Management, Power & Energy Monitoring, Power Automation & SCADA, Power Protection, and Fiber OpticSplicing, Termination, and Testing. Our solutions and services are vital to the Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power Generation, Industrial, and Commercial sectors across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Services

Comprehensive services from consultancy to full turnkey delivery.

Power Quality & Audit

Comprehensive services including Energy and Harmonics Audits, Analysis, and implementation of solutions for power quality improvement.

  • Energy & Power Quality Audits, Studies, and Analysis.
  • Power Factor Correction (PFC) Systems.
  • Harmonics Mitigation (Active & Passive Filters).
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) & Stabilizers.
  • VFD (Variable Frequency Drives) & Soft Starters.

Energy Management & Automation

Design, supply, and integration of SCADA and Power Monitoring Systems for control, billing, and automation solutions.

  • Electrical Power Monitoring Systems (EPMS/PMS).
  • SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition).
  • Power Systems Automation (PSA/Reduced PSA) for Aramco projects.
  • Remote Switching & Monitoring Systems (RSMS) for Aramco projects.

CCTV, Security & Comms

Full turnkey services for Security Systems, Communication infrastructure, and Smart Home Automation technologies.

  • CCTV (Surveillance cameras and remote monitoring).
  • Security Systems (e.g., Paradox security alarm system).
  • IP Telephone and Intercom Systems.
  • Communication & Internet (wired & wireless IT systems and Networking).
  • Smart Home Automation.

Electrical Supply & Cabling

Supply of specialized electrical panels and certified services for Fiber Optic and Copper cable splicing, termination, and testing.

  • Supply of Electrical Panels (Distribution, Synchronizing, ATS).
  • Transformers & Electrical Panels supply.
  • Fiber Optic Cable Splicing, Termination, and Testing (OTDR/Power Meter).
  • Design, Engineering, and Installation services for all systems.
