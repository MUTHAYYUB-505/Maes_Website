About MAES

MAES, is a technology Company dedicated to continuously enhance its competencies in CCTV & Security System, Energy Efficiency to address the pressing needs of consumers to have secured facility, reliable power and to conserve energy. To achieve these objectives, theCompany provides solutions and services utilizing Power Quality, Energy Management, Power & Energy Monitoring, Power Automation & SCADA, Power Protection, and Fiber OpticSplicing, Termination, and Testing.

Our solutions and services are vital to the Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power Generation, Industrial, and Commercial sectors across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Our Mission is providing high quality of services at most competitive price without compromising quality, safety and environment. When it comes to our services, we make sure that we keep in mind even a minute detail to serve our client better and maintain our good relation with them.
