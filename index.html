Our Commitment to Excellence

MAES is a leading Saudi-owned group of companies based in Al Khobar, KSA, focused on delivering advanced technology solutions in **CCTV & Security Systems** and **Energy Efficiency**.

Our specialized solutions are critical for the **Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power Generation, Industrial, and Commercial sectors**. We enhance facility security, ensure reliable power distribution, and drive significant energy conservation.

We differentiate ourselves by offering **complete engineering packages** and specialized services, operating as a single, reliable point of contact for complex, multi-disciplinary projects.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality services at the most competitive price, rigorously upholding standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To deliver cost-effective and high-quality solutions for Power Quality, Automation, and Security, ensuring the **lowest Cost of Ownership** and **Highest Yield & Uptime** for our customers.

Our Expertise: Comprehensive Solutions

Power Quality & Efficiency

Enhancing energy performance and reliability through specialized audits and mitigation systems.

  • Energy & Power Quality Audits and Studies.
  • Power Factor Correction (PFC) Systems.
  • Harmonics Mitigation (Active/Passive Filters).
  • UPS Systems & Stabilizers.

Energy Management & Automation
