About M.A.E.S

M. Advanced Energy Systems (MAES) is a leading provider of comprehensive electrical and energy solutions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With a commitment to reliability, efficiency, and cutting-edge technology, we help industrial, commercial, and utility sectors optimize their power infrastructure.

Our expert team delivers end-to-end services, from consultation and design to installation and maintenance, ensuring your operations run smoothly and safely. We are headquartered in Al Khobar with branches in Jubail, Riyadh, and Jeddah to serve you better.

Our Core Services

Power Quality Solutions

We provide comprehensive solutions for power quality issues like harmonics, poor power factor, and voltage sags, ensuring stable and clean power supply for sensitive equipment.

Industrial Automation and Metering

Offering advanced automation, SCADA systems, and smart metering solutions to optimize industrial processes, improve efficiency, and enable accurate energy consumption monitoring.

Industrial CCTV and Security Systems

Designing and installing robust security and surveillance systems, including industrial-grade CCTV and access control, to protect your assets and personnel.

Electrical Distribution Panels

Manufacturing and supplying custom-built electrical distribution and control panels (MDBs, SMDBs, MCCs) that adhere to the highest international safety and quality standards.
